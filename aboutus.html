About Us

We are a dedicated team working on an *AI-powered solution* to detect tuberculosis (TB) using *X-ray scans*. Our government-backed project is designed to provide *fast, accurate, and accessible diagnostics*.

About the Project

Implementing *AI-based applications* for tuberculosis (TB) patients can *revolutionize diagnosis, treatment, and monitoring*. AI can analyze *chest X-rays* with high accuracy, detect abnormalities, and help healthcare providers identify high-risk individuals.

In *resource-limited settings, AI-powered tools **bridge the gap* by providing a *cost-effective, scalable solution*. With *real-time analysis, AI can **assist healthcare workers* and *enhance public health strategies*, improving TB patient care worldwide.

How It Works

1. Upload Your X-ray: Click the button below to securely upload your chest X-ray image.

2. AI Analysis: Our AI model scans the image, detects TB-related patterns, and generates a *detailed report*.

3. Receive Results: Get *instant feedback* on TB detection, along with *medical recommendations*.
